Classically trained pianist, Hannah is establishing herself as an artist of rich potential, technical fluency and musical awareness on stage in her concerts and projects. Hannah holds the Bachelor and Master degrees in piano performance with honored full scholarship and the K12 certified music teacher by New Jersey State. She also served as a teaching artist at New Jersey Performance Arts Center in 2017. Hannah has been aÂ prize winner in numerous competitions including the International Piano Competition and usually appeared as a soloist throughout the world, including has performed at Carnegie Hall, Steinway & Sons Hall, Lincoln Center Performing Arts Center.

Committed to reaching younger generation of musicians, Hannah presents concerts and leads education outreach programs in New Jersey Public Schools. She has served as a teaching artist at New Jersey Performance Arts Center. With her over 15 years of music teaching experience, she has trained several students to win some competitive competitions and further the opportunities to perform at Carnegie Hall. Many her students passed the ABRSM EXAM (The Associated Board of the Royal Schools of Music) with Distinctions.
