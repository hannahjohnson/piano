Hannah is currently pursuing a Master degree at New Jersey City University with a full scholarship. She has performed at many concert series since her early career. Her principle teachers were Min Kim, Ning An and Gloria Chien. Also, Hannah has participated in master classes and Lessons with numerous concert pianists include Jon Nakamastu, Amy Yang and Lang Lang. She has won numerous competitions and received several significant awards. With the guidance of multiple well-known pianists, she has developed fantastic skills not just to perform but also to teach. In addition to her performance career, she currently serves as a teaching artists at New Jersey Performance Arts Center. With many experience’s in teaching. She has appeared as a guest lecturer and master class presenter at several festivals.
