Hannah is currently pursuing a master degree at New Jersey City University with a full scholarship. She has performed at many concert series since her early career. Her principal teachers include Min Kim, Ning An and Gloria Chien. Also, Hannah has participated in master classes and lessons with several well-known concert pianists including Jon Nakamastu, Amy Yang and Lang Lang. Over the past 15 years, she has won numerous competitions and received several significant awards.

Hannah is more than a pianist: she is also a skilled piano teacher for over ten years. In addition to her performance career, she currently serves as a teaching artist at New Jersey Performance Arts Center. In fact, she has been a guest lecturer and master class presenter at severalfestivals.
